## Intro Paragraph 1
At Serving Society, we understand that some NDIS participants have complex medical needs that require specialised, high-level nursing care. Our **High Intensity Nursing Support** service is designed to provide safe, compassionate, and professional clinical care that enables you to live with dignity, comfort, and independence — right where you feel most at home.

## Intro Paragraph 2
Our experienced support team works closely with registered nurses and healthcare professionals to deliver tailored care plans that address your specific health needs. Whether you require ongoing clinical management, post-hospitalisation support, or complex wound care, we are committed to being by your side every step of the way.

## Our Support Includes
- Wound care assessment and management
- Medication administration and management
- Enteral (tube) feeding support
- Tracheostomy care and airway management
- Diabetes monitoring and management
- Catheter care and continence support
- Palliative and end-of-life care
- Stoma care and management
- Subcutaneous and IV medication support
- Post-surgical recovery support

## Closing Paragraph
We believe every person deserves access to high-quality clinical support that respects their individual goals and preferences. Our team works in collaboration with your existing healthcare providers, family, and support network to deliver seamless, holistic care that keeps you safe and well in your own environment.
