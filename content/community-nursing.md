## Intro Paragraph 1
At Serving Society, we bring professional nursing care directly to you — in your home, your community, and wherever you feel most comfortable. Our **Community Nursing Support** service is built around your individual health needs, helping you manage your conditions confidently and independently without the need for repeated hospital visits.

## Intro Paragraph 2
Our team collaborates with registered nurses, your GP, allied health professionals, and your personal support network to develop a comprehensive care plan tailored specifically to your goals. We focus on building your skills, confidence, and independence so you can take greater control of your own health and wellbeing.

## Our Support Includes
- In-home wound care and dressing changes
- Medication management and administration
- Continence assessment and support
- Respiratory care and assistance
- Catheter management and monitoring
- Diabetes education and management
- Palliative and end-of-life care coordination
- Health monitoring and clinical assessments
- Care coordination with family and primary carers
- Education and training for participants and carers

## Closing Paragraph
We are passionate about enabling NDIS participants to remain active, engaged, and connected within their communities. Our community nursing team brings clinical expertise with a human touch — ensuring every interaction is respectful, empowering, and focused on what matters most to you.
